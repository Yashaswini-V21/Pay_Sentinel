**Startup-Grade Pitch | BLUEPRINT 2026**

---

## 1. THE PROBLEM
*   **The Human Story**: Manjunath, a 52-year-old kirana owner in Malleshwaram, just lost ₹12,000—his entire week’s margin—to a "structuring" fraudster while his shop was crowded.
*   **The Moment of Pain**: He saw a "Payment Successful" screen on the customer's phone, but the money never arrived. By the time he checked his bank app 20 minutes later, the fraudster was long gone.
*   **The Failure**: Existing tools are "Black Boxes" that send delayed English alerts. For 6.5 Crore Kannada speakers, these systems are effectively silent, leaving them defenseless against high-speed UPI attacks.

---

## 2. THE SOLUTION
**Elevator Pitch**: PaySentinel is an AI-first, real-time UPI fraud detection layer that protects small merchants from inventory loss using sub-100ms anomaly detection and context-aware Kannada voice alerts.

*   **Zero-Label Intelligence**: Powered by a Hybrid Isolation Forest ensemble that learns a merchant's "unique business fingerprint" without needing historical fraud data.
*   **Explainable Defense**: It doesn't just say "Fraud"; it explains *why* in Kannada ("Ramesh, this is 7x larger than your usual morning sales") using SHAP-powered explainability.
*   **High-Fidelity Interface**: A "Stark Tech" dashboard designed for instant trust, providing visual and audio feedback that works even in noisy, busy environments.
